How to Buy

1. Set up a crypto wallet on the ETH network

You can use Trust Wallet or Metamask, these are the two we recommend.

2. Deposit Ethereum (ETH) to ETH

Visit Uniswap, connect your wallet, and select the amount of ETH you want to trade. Ensure to keep enough ETH for gas fees.

3. Swap ETH for $GINNAN

Head over to Uniswap and paste the GINNAN contract address listed on this website to swap your ETH.

4. Congrats, you're a GINNAN

Now all you have to do is add the contract address to your wallet for your GINNAN tokens to show. Welcome aboard!
